üî• Smash Techniques to Win Every Rally

Jump Smash

A powerful overhead smash hit mid-air for a steep angle and extra force.

Steep Smash

Smash at a sharp angle downwards to restrict opponent's movement.

Cross-Court Smash

Diagonal smashes to push opponents off-balance and create space.

Deceptive Smash

Disguised smashes confuse opponents and force weak returns.

Smash & Follow-Up

Combo move: smash and rush forward to finish at the net.

Body Smash

Aimed directly at opponent's body to limit their response options.
